About EBEB GRACEWILL SCHOOL

EBEB GRACEWILL SCHOOL is a reputable institution located at 60, Inawole Quarters, near Liberty Hospital, Adaba, Awotan, Apete, Ibadan. We are committed to providing high-quality education that builds character, inspires creativity, and prepares students for a bright future.

Our mission is to nurture excellence through sound moral values, innovative teaching, and personalized learning experiences. With a dedicated staff and modern learning environment, we empower every child to reach their full potential.
